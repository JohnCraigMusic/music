John Craig

Online Piano & Theory Teacher

My name is John Craig. I am a pianist from Northern Ireland with over a decade of experience playing the piano. I have achieved Grade 5 in piano and Grade 3 in music theory. I have gained valuable experience performing in front of large crowds and participating in various musical events. I’m also a member of the Ulster Youth Jazz Orchestra. Next year I will be going on to study Music Production and Performance at Southern Regional College.

I offer online piano lessons over Zoom. The first 5 half-hour lessons will be free, allowing you to determine if they suit you.
