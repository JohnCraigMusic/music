About Me

Are you looking for a great way to learn to play piano? If you already have access to a keyboard or piano, we offer you the opportunity to learn live and online from the comfort of your own home. There's no need to travel, and we deliver a professional and tailored tutoring service for all ages.

JC Music is operated by a young, enthusiastic music student based in Northern Irlenad, offering lessons from beginner to grade 3. Our lesson sare competitively priced, allowing students to develop their musical interest without significant costs or major time commitment.

You can book now by completing out contact form. We are happy to setup an initial zoom chat to answer your questions.

For younger children, we recommend that parents take part in the calls and become involved in the learning experience.
